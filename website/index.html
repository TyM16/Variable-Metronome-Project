Variable Metronome Project - VMP


VMP is the metronome for the modern musician, right on your phone or computer. It's goal is to eliminate the need to interrupt practice to update your device's time keeping.


At it's core we keep it simple. Just give the timing you want the app to follow via direct input or audio file. No complex functions, just you and your music without needless hassle.


The variable metronome will have functionality to interpret various audio file type's timing and translate it into a concise, auto transitioning session.


We hope to implement functionality so that you can access your favorite music platforms such as Spotify to train timing from various artists, all within one app.
